One of the Dark Days

2019-04-02

I fall too soon and too deep Into waters I am unaware of. And I forget to swim When I need to. I inhale the water Knowing all well, that it isn't well. And then I cry, Try to find faults In others when I am wrong, And in myself when others have wronged. I find myself At crossroads Where every road is a bad omen. And yet, I have to choose one And I go on blindly Trusting That it will all be alright When nothing actually is Alright. I am as broken As I am perfect, Perfect imperfections. The more I know The less I understand. It feels like I am climbing Falling stairs. I try to find the key Key to success Key to happiness Key to satisfaction That I have lost Long ago, beyond recovery I try to have hope That I will find it Because Hope is all I have, and I don't want to be locked in. I try too much And do too little.

Written on one of the dark days.
